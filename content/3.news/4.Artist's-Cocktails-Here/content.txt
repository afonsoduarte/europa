We've created the menu and identity for a bar at [Documenta 13](http://d13.documenta.de/). *Artists' Cocktails Here* is a bar by artists Ryan Gander and Mario Garcia Torres. The menu includes recipes for cocktails created by artists like Fiona Banner, Jeremy Deller and David Shrigley. It's also available in the winter 2012/2013 issue of [032c Magazine](http://032c.com/).
